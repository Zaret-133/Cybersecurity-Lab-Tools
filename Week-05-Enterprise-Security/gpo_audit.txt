==================================================
SESSION 14: GROUP POLICY AUDIT REPORT
==================================================

Question 1: What does LSDOU stand for?

Answer: Local, Site, Domain, and Organizational Unit.

Question 2: In what order are Group Policies processed?

Answer: They are processed in the order of Local first, then Site, then Domain, and finally Organizational Unit (OU).

Question 3: Which policy takes precedence if there is a conflict?

Answer: The policy closest to the user—the Organizational Unit (OU)—takes precedence and wins in a conflict.
